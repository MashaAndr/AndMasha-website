Passing

The project is the experience of overcoming nostalgia. An attempt to tribute the past. To thank the past, childhood and roots for guidance in life. To make its life continue in the present, in an altered form. Project final form is a personal calendar that uses shapes of foil seals to indicate time. These shapes are taken from monthly tickets from a small town on north-east of Russia called Cherepovets. This 'time tracking' technique, unlike numbers, create a unique composition for each day, week, and year.
